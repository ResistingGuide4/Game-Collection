De Morgan's Laws

De Morgan's Laws, named after Augustus are some rules that state that a NOR gate is the same as negating the inputs of an AND gate, and a NAND gate is the same as negating the inputs of an OR gate. If that doesn't make sense, don't worry. I made a diagram to help you visualize.
